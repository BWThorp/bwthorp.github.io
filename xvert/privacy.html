Privacy Policy

XVert  ·  Last updated April 7, 2026

Overview

XVert does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

Data Storage

All conversions, custom categories, and Formulas are stored locally on your device. No data is ever sent to any server operated by Brigg Thorp or any third party.

Analytics & Tracking

XVert does not use any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or third-party tracking tools. No usage data or crash reports are collected beyond what Apple provides to developers through App Store Connect.

Children's Privacy

XVert does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
