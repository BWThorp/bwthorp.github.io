Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert a unit?

Select a category from the home grid, enter a value in any field, and the other fields update instantly.

What is a Formula?

Formulas let you define your own conversions using a custom equation — useful for things like cooking ratios, fuel economy, or any calculation that isn't a standard unit conversion.

How do I create a Formula?

Tap the Formula tab, then tap the + button. Give it a name, define your input and output labels, and enter your equation. Use the variable x to represent the input value.

Can I reorder categories?

Yes — long-press any category tile on the home grid to enter edit mode, then drag them into any order you like.

How do I change decimal precision?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and adjust the Decimal Places slider to your preference.
