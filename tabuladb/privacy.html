Privacy Policy

TabulaDB  ·  Last updated April 7, 2026

Overview

TabulaDB is built with a privacy-first approach. Your data belongs to you and stays under your control.

Data Storage

All databases, records, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is ever sent to any server operated by Brigg Thorp.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, TabulaDB may sync your data to your private iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit. This sync occurs between your own devices only. Brigg Thorp does not have access to your iCloud data.

In-App Purchases

TabulaDB offers a one-time Pro upgrade processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. TabulaDB does not receive or store any payment information.

Analytics & Tracking

TabulaDB does not use any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or third-party tracking tools. No usage data or crash reports are collected beyond what Apple provides to developers through App Store Connect.

Children's Privacy

TabulaDB does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
