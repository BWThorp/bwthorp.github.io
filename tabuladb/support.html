Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new database?

Tap the + button on the main screen. Choose a template to get started quickly, or start from scratch and add your own fields.

What field types are supported?

TabulaDB supports text, number, date, checkbox, picklist, and image fields. You can mix and match any combination when designing your database.

How do I restore a Pro purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings within TabulaDB and tap Restore Purchase. As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID, your Pro access will be restored at no charge.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings and tap Export Backup. This creates a .tabulabackup file you can save anywhere — AirDrop it to your Mac, save it to Files, or store it in iCloud Drive.

Does TabulaDB sync across my devices?

Yes, if you have iCloud enabled. Your databases sync automatically through your private iCloud account. Nothing is shared with any third party.

How do I export records to CSV?

Open a database, tap the share icon in the toolbar, and choose Export as CSV. The file can be opened in Numbers, Excel, or any spreadsheet app.
